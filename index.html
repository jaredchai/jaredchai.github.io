About me:

I am currently an associate in the Quantitative Finance & Economics (QFE) team at EY-Parthenon in New York City.

I graduated from Georgia Institute of Technology in 2023 with an M.S. in Computational Science & Engineering and an M.S. in Quantitative & Computational Finance.

I graduated Summa Cum Laude and High Distinction from University of Michigan in 2021 with a B.B.A. from the Stephen M. Ross School of Business, a B.S.E. from the Department of Industrial & Operations Engineering, and a minor in Computer Science.

Passed CFA Level 1 (May 2024, top 10%, 46% pass rate), CFA Level 2 (November 2024, top 10%, 39% pass rate); CFA Level 3 Candidate.
